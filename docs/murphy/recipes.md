# Recipes

## Teresa's banana bread

*adapted from [this recipe](https://www.foodnetwork.com/recipes/banana-bread-recipe-1969572)*

### ingredients

- 2 sticks softened butter *or* 1 cup oil
  - To soften the butter, set it out until reaches approximately room temperature *or* microwave it for 20-30 seconds
- ~2.25 cups sugar
  - If the bananas are very very mushy, then they'll be extra watery when mashed, so use white sugar
  - Otherwise (or if you like more moist banana bread), use brown sugar
- 4 eggs
- 6-8 bananas, mashed
  - Making banana bread is a great way to reduce Banana Lounge food waste :wink:
  - The more bananas you use, the less bread-y (dry) and more cake-y (moist) your banana bread will be
- 2 tsps baking powder
- 2 tsps baking soda
- 2 tsps salt
- 3-4 tbps cinnamon
- 1-2 tsp vanilla extract
- 4 cups flour
- (optional) a metric ton of chocolate chips

### instructions

1. Combine ingredients in the order listed
2. Pour into a 9"x13" casserole dish and bake at 325° for ~65 min *or* at 350° for ~30 min; you'll know when it's done if you stick in a toothpick or chopstick and it comes out clean (i.e., no raw banana bread goop attached)
3. Email out to hall to share banana bread with your fellow Beasties :yum:
