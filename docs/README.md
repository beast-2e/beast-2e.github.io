Second East, monoymously known as Beast, is a hall that is **first and foremost about people**. Hall culture is defined by all of our **diverse and varied** hall members, so it's always changing and evolving. Beast's current range of interests include:

- **baking** (banana bread),
- **cooking** (so many dumplings),
- **playing music** (and KARAOKE!!),
- **reading discussions** (beast.. reading.. breading? wait.),
- **✧･ﾟ✧･shenanigans･ﾟ✧･ﾟ✧** (no door can stop us now >:) hehehe),
- ... and much more!

Beast is home to **36 human residents**, 2 lovely canine residents, the **Very Large LED Array (VLLA)**, a pretty nifty **water filter**, and beautiful **murals** including one of our mascot, The Beast. Beast's glounge is often filled with people psetting, playing board games, or just hanging about. It's home to the aformentined VLLA, along with our **Free Food Table (FFT)** where people can leave food (or other things) for others to take. Our walounge has couches, foofs, a hammock, and a projector to watch movies, TV, or **whatever your heart desires**.

Whatever your interests are, there’s always people around who are down to do things with you. Beast is a tight-knit hall and welcoming to all, so **no matter who you are, you can find a place here.**
