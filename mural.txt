--- This policy was ratified on SEPTEMBER 10, 2006 and should not be altered unless a hall vote agrees on specific changes that should be instituted ---



NEW MURALS
- requires 15+ signatures in approval
- you may vote yes or no; abstentions need not sign
- 2/3 majority of signatures must be in approval
- there is a seven day period in which the petition will remain active, after which it is void

PAINTING OVER OLD MURALS
- requires a 2/3 majority of approvals from ALL hall members
- petition will remain active for seven days
    - if hall members have not signed within that period they will be directly notified by email
    - if after another three days, hall members have not made a decision, they will be counted as abstentions, and their votes will not count toward the total
- petition period must be within term dates
	- not during finals or reading period
	- not during IAP
- yes, no, and abstentions should all sign in this scenario

DOORS
- hall chairs must be contacted before the door is painted over, and if the artist still lives on the hall, they must also be contacted 

UNAPPROVED MURALS
- murals put up without consent are not subject to normal rules, and as such they may be painted over at any time
- these murals may be post-approved, if the above rules are followed

All murals painted before the ratification of these rules are considered Approved Murals and are automatically subject to the above rules.
